Nut'n For You Boo!!!

Instructions

Ingredients

Part 1: Making Blueberry Ice Cream


Over medium heat, warm the blueberries, water and citrus juice until it starts to boil. Strain mixture, saving both


the liquid portion. Let cool to room temperature.


In a cold sauce pan, crack eggs into pan. Add cherry liquid into the sauce pan. Over low heat warm the egg and blueberry


combination. Using a hand whisk, constantly whisk. Keep whisking until the batter becomes like a custard consistency.


Slowly add to the whipped cream. Slowly add the blueberry sauce. Combine thoroughly.


*To cut time, add the cut up berries, juices and water to smoothie machine or juicer.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in blueberry combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Part 2: Almond Butter Ice Cream


In a bowl, pour rum, milk and almond butter. Heat in the microwave for about 1 and a half minutes. Whisk together


until thoroughly combined. Let cool.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy.


Add in almond butter combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Once the Almond portion is done, scoop over the Blueberry Ice Cream waiting in the freezer. Fold Ice Cream until you get


a good marbling look.


Part 1: Blueberry Ice Cream

1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


4 cups blueberries


1 freshly squeezed orange


1 tablespoon lemon juice


1 1/2 cups water


Part 2: Almond Butter Ice Cream

1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1 cup Shaved Almonds


1 cup Almond Butter


1/2 cup Spiced Rum
