Kahlua & Amaretto Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, and vanilla. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy.


Add Kahlua and milk and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into ice cream machine. Churn according to Ice Cream Machine instructions.


Pour ice cream into a large bowl. Fold in chopped Almonds and fudge. Place in freezer to set for about 2 hours.


Scoop and serve.


1 1/2 cups Roasted Almonds (Chopped)


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup Whole Milk


3/4 cup Kahlua Liquor


2 cups Fudge Sauce


1 tablespoon Vanilla Extract
