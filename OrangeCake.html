Best Orange Cake

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Butter a 9 inch round cake pan. Flour the buttered pan.


Sift together the flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.


In a different bowl, add melted butter, oil, sugar and beat until thoroughly combined.


Add eggs one at a time, beating the mixture after adding each egg.


Add milk and whisk. Add in Orange Juice, zest and vanilla. Whisk for about a minute.


Add the dry ingredients to the wet. Gently mix for a few seconds. There might be lumps in the batter, just ignore but


don't over mix the batter. The lumps will dissolve and won't be seen in the cake after it is baking.


Pour prepared cake batter into the previously prepared cake pan.


Bake for about 40 - 45 minutes or until it's golden and a skewer inserted in the middle of the cake comes out clean.


Let the cake cool for a few minutes before removing from the cake pan. Place cake on a cooling rack.


Mix frosting. Frosting will be very thin. Brush frosting over top of cake while cooling. The frosting will soak into the cake.


Cut and serve


1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour


1/2 teaspoon salt


2 teaspoon baking powder


1 1/4 cup powdered sugar


1/2 cup milk


1/2 cup orange juice


1/4 cup oil


1/4 cup melted butter


3 medium eggs


1 tablespoon orange zest


1 teaspoon vanilla


Frosting:

1/2 cup powdered sugar


1/8 cup water or milk
