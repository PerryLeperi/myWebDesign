Chewy Brownie Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and line baking sheets with parchment paper.


In a medium size bowl, sift together flour, cocoa, baking soda and baking powder. Stir in sugar and salt. Mix until well


combined.


Beat in the butter and vanilla. Add in the eggs until combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.


Scoop dough into tablespoonfuls on the lined cookie sheet about 2 inches apart.


Bake for about 8 - 10 minutes and remove from oven. Allow the cookies to cool before transferring them to wire cooling rack.


1 cup all purpose flour


1/2 cup dark cocoa powder


1/2 teaspoon baking powder


1 teaspoon baking soda


2/3 cup granulated sugar


1/2 cup butter (room temperature)


1 teaspoon salt


1 teaspoon vanilla


2 large eggs


1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
