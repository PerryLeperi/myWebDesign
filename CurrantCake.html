Currant Cake

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.


For the Cake:

Prepare in a 9 inch spring-form cake pan by greasing it with butter and set aside.


In a large mixing bowl, using hand-held mixer, beat together butter, zest, and sugar until light and fluffy.


Add the eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, ground cloves, and brandy.


In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Add flour mixture to wet ingredients until fully


incorporated. Add red currants, almond slivers, candied lemon peel until combined.


Pour into prepared pan and set aside.


For the Crumb Topping:

Add all ingredients except the currants in a mixing bowl and mix together with your hands, pressing the butter into the


other ingredients until a crumbly mixture is formed.


Loosely crumble this mixture over the cake with currants (optional).


Bake for 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.


Once you remove the cake from the oven, it is very important to leave the cake in the pan. Place cake in pan on cooling rack


for about 30 minutes.


Remove cake from pan and allow to fully cool before serving.


For the cake:

1/2 cup butter, softened


2 cups all purpose flour


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups currants


1 medium sized lemon's worth of zest


1/4 cup candied lemon peel


2 1/2 tablespoons slivered/sliced almonds


2 large eggs


1/8 cup brandy


1/2 cup milk


1/2 tablespoon cinnamon


1/3 teaspoon ground cloves


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


Pinch of salt


For the Crumb Topping:


1/4 cup butter, softened


1/4 cup brown sugar, lightly packed


1/4 cup granulated sugar


1/4 cup all purpose flour


1/2 cup quick oats


1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon


1/4 cup red currants (optional)
