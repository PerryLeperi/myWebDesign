Chocolate Cream Cheese Pie

Instructions

Ingredients

Crust:


Crush all the Oreos in a ziplock bag or put them in the food processor until they are crumbs with no chunks.


Pour crumbs into mixing bowl and add peanut butter and honey. Mix thoroughly.


Pour crust paste into pie tin and spread evenly. Place in refrigerator for about 30 minutes.


Pour pie filling into pie crust and spread evenly.


Filling:

In a small microwave safe mixing bowl, heat chocolate chips and milk for about 2 minutes. Whisk until chips are melted.


In a large bowl, beat together sugar and cream cheese.


Add chocolate mixture and beat until smooth.


Fold in whipped topping to blend.


Spoon mixture into pie crust. Place in the refrigerator to chill for about 4 hours.


1 package Oreo cookie


1 cup peanut butter (creamy)


1/4 honey


3 cups semisweet chocolate chips


1/2 cup whole milk


2 tablespoons granulated sugar


8 ounces cream cheese (softened)


8 ounce container frozen non-dairy topping (thawed)
