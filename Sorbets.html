Sorbets

Instructions

Ingredients

Place all ingredients into a large bowl, mix until sugar dissolves. Place in the refrigerator for about 2 hours. Pour

into the food processor until pureed. Transfer into Ice Cream Maker and freeze according to Ice Cream Maker's instructions.


Sorbet's are fun and easy. Though the process is not tedious, the flavors will have outstanding results. One

thing about Sorbet, is there is no dairy. If you like making smoothies, then you'll have a knack for making

any sorbet.

The following is what I use for a basic Sorbet:


1 pound fresh cut fruit


1/2 cup granulated sugar


2 tablespoons corn syrup


2 1/2 cups cold water
