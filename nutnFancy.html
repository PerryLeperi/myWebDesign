Part 1: Apple Ice Cream


Over medium heat, warm the apples, water and cinnamon until it starts to boil. Strain apple and mash the apple chunks into an


applesauce consistency. Add the apple liquid to the apple sauce and set off to the side. Let cool to room temperature.


In a cold sauce pan, crack eggs into pan. Add apple portion into the sauce pan. Over low heat warm the egg and apple


combination. Using a hand whisk, constantly whisk. Keep whisking until the batter becomes like a custard consistency.


Combine thoroughly.


*To cut time, add the cut up apples, and water to smoothie machine or juicer.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in apple combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Part 2: Peanut Butter Ice Cream


In a microwave safe bowl, add milk and peanut butter. Heat in the microwave for about 2 and a half minutes. Whisk together


until thoroughly combined. Add spiced rum and mix thoroughly and set aside.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed to high.


Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in peanut butter combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Once the peanut butter portion is done, scoop over the Apple Ice Cream waiting in the freezer. Fold Ice Cream until you


get a good marbling look.


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup granulated sugar


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1 cup chopped peanuts


1 cup Peanut Butter


1/2 cup Spiced Rum


1/2 cup milk


4 apples diced


1 teaspoon cinnamon


3 Large eggs


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup granulated sugar
