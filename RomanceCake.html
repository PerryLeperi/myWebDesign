Passionate Romance Cake

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.


For the Cake:

Grease two 9 inch round cake pans and line bottoms with parchment paper.


In a large bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.


In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla until well combined.

Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until smooth. Carefully stir in the boiling water until

the batter is well combined.


Gently fold in the fresh blackberries.


Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick test comes out clean.


Cool pan on wire rack for about 10 minutes, the transfer one cake to cake platter.


For the Buttercream:

Beat the butter until creamy. Gradually add powdered sugar, one cup at a time, beating on low until combined,. Add food


coloring to color likeness. Increase to high and beat until fluffy.


Add blackberry puree and vanilla, beating until the frosting is smooth and evenly colored.


For the Ganache:

Heat the cream, corn syrup, and vanilla until it just begins to simmer, then pour over chocolate chips. Let sit for about

2 minutes, then stir until smooth. Let cool until it thickens slightly but can still be poured.


Assemble the Cake:

Trim the tops of the cakes if needed to level them. Place one layer on your cake platter and spread with berry buttercream.


Top with second cake layer.


Frost the outside of the cake with thin layer of the buttercream for the crumb coat. Apply a final layer of buttercream.


Smooth and level the frosting.


Pour the ganache over the top of the cake, allowing some to drip down the sides.


Decorate with fresh berries.


For the cake:

1 3/4 cups all purpose flour


3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder


2 cups granulated sugar


2 teaspoons baking powder


1/2 teaspoon baking soda


1/2 teaspoon salt


2 large eggs, room temperature


1 cup whole milk, room temperature


1/2 cup vegetable oil


2 teaspoons vanilla extract


1 cup boiling water


1 1/2 cups fresh black berries


For the Buttercream:

1 cup butter softened


4 cups powdered sugar, sifted


1/2 cup pureed blackberries (strained to remove seeds)


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


Purple food coloring


For the Ganache:

1 cup heavy cream


1 cup dark chocolate chips


2 tablespoons light corn syrup or honey


1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract


For Decoration:

Fresh blackberries, raspberries, elderberries.
