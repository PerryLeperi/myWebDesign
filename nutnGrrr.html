Ingredients

Part 1: Make Raspberry Ice Cream


Over medium heat, warm the raspberries and water until it starts to boil. Strain mixture, saving both the liquid


portion. Let cool to room temperature.


In a cold sauce pan, crack eggs into pan. Add raspberry liquid into the sauce pan. Over low heat warm the egg and


raspberry combination. Using a hand whisk, constantly whisk. Continue whisking until the batter becomes like a custard


consistency. Slowly add the raspberry sauce to the whipped cream. Combine thoroughly. After pouring the Raspberry portion


into the Ice Cream Maker, churn ice cream as directed in Ice Cream directions.


Start second part of ice cream


Part 2: Sunflower Seed Butter Ice Cream


In a bowl, place the milk and sunflower seed butter. Heat in the microwave for about 1 and a half minutes. Add rum and


mix thoroughly.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer


speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in sunflower combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour sunflower batter into Ice Cream maker, and make according to Ice Cream Maker's instructions.


Once the sunflower portion is done, scoop over the raspberry ice cream waiting in the freezer. Fold Ice Cream until you


get a good marbling look.


Part 1:Raspberry Ice Cream


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 teaspoon of vanilla extract


2 pints raspberries


3 large eggs


1 cup water


Part 2: Sunflower Seed Butter Ice Cream


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup granulated sugar


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1 cup Sunflower Seed Butter


1/2 cup Spiced Rum


1/2 cup milk
