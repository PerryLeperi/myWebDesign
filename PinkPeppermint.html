Pink Peppermint Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Only if you are using mint leaves, you should prepare as follows: In a pot, pour milk, red food coloring and mint leaves.


Warm until the whole house smells minty fresh!!! Or until the mint batter reaches 150 degrees.


Strain into separate bowl and let cool to room temperature.


If you're using extract, you can skip this process and pour the ingredients in with the batter.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in mint combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour into ice cream mixing churn and make according to the ice cream maker's instructions.


Add in crushed candy canes during the last 2 minutes of churning.


1/2 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1 teaspoon peppermint extract or 6 mint leaves


2 cups crushed peppermint candy canes (pour into churn the last 2 minutes before complete churned)


1/2 teaspoon salt


5 - 6 drops of red food coloring


1 cup milk
