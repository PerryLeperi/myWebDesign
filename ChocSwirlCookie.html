Chocolate Swirl Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

This cookie is made in two portions


First Portion

Line baking sheets with parchment paper.


In a medium bowl sift together the flour, cocoa, baking soda, and baking powder. Stir in sugar and salt. Mix thoroughly.


Beat in the butter and vanilla. Then mix in the eggs.


Take 2 pieces of parchment paper - about 24 inches in length. Pour dough onto parchment paper and press down making it


as flat as possible. Put the second sheet of parchment paper over the top. Using a rolling pin, roll out dough to be


about 1/4 inch thickness and set aside.


Second Portion


In a medium size bowl, cream together chocolate chips, peanut butter, cream cheese, sugars and vanilla extract with a mixer.


Add in an egg one at a time, mixing thoroughly between each egg added.


In a separate bowl, mix together flour, salt and baking soda. Once mixed, little by little, add flour combination with peanut


butter combination. Mix until all is combined.


Roll out dough the same as Chocolate portion. Carefully removed the top piece of parchment paper from both doughs. Place both


doughs parallel to each other and carefully roll one dough onto the other.


Roll out dough one more time. Remove the top parchment paper. Roll both doughs into a log, removing dough from the last piece


of parchment paper. Now roll log onto parchment and place in freezer for about 20 minutes.


Preheat oven to 350 degrees.


Unroll parchment paper from dough log. Cut into 1/4 inch disks. Place on lined cookie sheet about 1 1/2 inches apart for about


10 minutes. Let cookie cool on pan before transferring to cooling rack.


First Portion

1 cup all purpose flour


1/2 cup dark cocoa powder


1/2 teaspoon baking powder


1 teaspoon baking soda


2/3 cup granulated sugar


1/2 cup butter softened


1/2 teaspoon salt


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


2 large eggs


Second Portion

1 cup (8 ounces) cream cheese (softened)


1 cup peanut butter


3/4 cup brown sugar (packed)


1/4 cup granulated sugar


1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract


2 Large Eggs (room temperature)


2 1/4 cups sifted all-purpose flour


You can anyone of these substitutes for Peanut Butter:

Almond Butter

Sunflower Seed Butter

Brazil Nut Butter

Cashew Butter

Hazelnut Butter
