Chocolate Sauce

Instructions

Ingredients

Heat cream in saucepan over medium heat for about 2 to 3 minutes while stirring continuously.


Remove from heat, add the dark chocolate and butter. And mix wee to get a smooth sauce. Ready to serve. Store in


refrigerator and use as needed.


1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream


1 3/4 cups chopped dark chocolate


1 teaspoon butter
