Praline Pecan Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Stir together 1/4 cup granulated sugar, brown sugar, butter, milk and corn syrup in a heavy saucepan. Bring to boil


over medium heat, stirring constantly. About 6 - 7 minutes or until the candy thermometer registers 234 degrees.


Remove from heat and vigorously stir in pecans.


Spoon pecan mixture onto wax paper. Let cool for about an hour. Break up pecans into tiny crumb pieces.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla, cinnamon and honey. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the


mixer speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy.


Add in Caramel sauce. Combine thoroughly.


Make ice cream according to Ice Cream Maker's instructions.


Add Praline Pecans into churned Ice cream 2 minutes before the end of the Ice Cream Churning is done.


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1/2 cup Caramel sauce


1/4 cup granulated sugar


1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar


2 tablespoons butter


2 1/2 tablespoons milk


2 teaspoons corn syrup


2 cups toasted pecan halves
