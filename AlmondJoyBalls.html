Almond Joy Balls

Instructions

Ingredients

Combine sugar, vanilla, salt, coconut, and condensed milk thoroughly.


Scrape down bowl. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and place in refrigerator for about 1 hour.


In medium size bowl add chocolate and whipping cream. Microwave in 20 second intervals, mixing between until smooth


and all chips are melted.


Using a table teaspoon, scoop a spoonful of coconut mixture. Press an almond into the coconut and form into a ball.


Dip ball into melted chocolate and place on parchment paper. Let the candies dry and harden before eating otherwise it'll


look like you have poop on your hands.


1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


Pinch of salt


2 cups powdered sugar


4 cups shredded sweetened coconut


80 whole toasted almonds


4 cups chocolate chips


4 tablespoons Heavy Whipping Cream
