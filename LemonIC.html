Lemon Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

In a medium-sized saucepan, whisk together yokes, lemon juice, lemon curd, brown sugar and salt over medium


heat until sugar is dissolved.


Whisk in milk and cream until combined. Add in lemon peels.


Bring heat to medium-high and bring to a simmer for about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature


(about an hour).


Strain mixture through a metal sieve into a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and chill for about 4 hours or over night.


Pour batter into ice cream maker. Make ice cream according to ice cream machine instructions.


6 egg yokes


1/2 cup lemon juice


1/4 cup lemon curd


3/4 cup brown sugar


1 teaspoon salt


1 cup whole milk


2 cups heavy whipping cream


2 lemons, peeled
