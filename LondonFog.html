London Fog Lavender Cake

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

*If you have Earl Grey tea bags, simply open the tea bags, if contents is course-grind down to a fine texture.


In a medium size bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined. Set aside.


Using the stand mixer fitted with paddle attachment or a hand mixer, cream together butter, Earl Grey tea and sugar on


medium-high until pale and fluffy - this usually takes about 2-3 minutes. Reduce the speed of your mixer and add eggs one


at a time until fully incorporated. Add in vanilla.


Turn off mixer and slowly add in flour and milk until thoroughly combined. Add in lavender until completely incorporated.


Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean. If not, continue baking for 5 minute


intervals until done.


Place cake in pan on wire rack for about 10 minutes before allowing to completely cool.


Remove cake from pan, placing on cake plate. Mix the frosting ingredients completely. Frosting will be very thin. Brush


frosting over the entire top of cake.


2 1/4 cup all purpose flour


2 teaspoons baking powder


4 Earl Grey Tea bags *(loose and finely ground)


3/4 teaspoon salt


3/4 cup butter, room temperature


1 1/2 cups granulated sugar


3 large eggs, room temperature


1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract


1 cup milk, room temperature


1 cup lavender, finely chopped


Frosting:

1 cup powdered sugar


1/4 cup warm water
