Peppernuts (Pfeffernusse Cookies)

Instructions

Ingredients

Heat and Combine the syrup, molasses, brown sugar and lard until the lard is melted. Let it cool.


When batch is cold, add in the following: eggs, ginger, cloves, dissolved baking soda, Anise, nuts or seeds and flour.


Add in enough flour to make a stiff dough. Roll into logs about the size of a nickel. Place rolls on a parchment lined


cookie sheet then place in the freezer until you want to bake them. Or you can put them in the refrigerator overnight.


Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for about 8 - 10 minutes.


With each log, cut cookies about 1/2 inch thick. Place on cookie sheet evenly. You can place the cookies fairly close


together as these do not spread too much when baking. These are great for when drinking coffee. They are often used for


dunking in coffee as it will help soften them.


1 pint Dark Syrup


1 pint Molasses


1 pound brown sugar


2 cups of lard


2 eggs


1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger


1 1/2 cloves


2 teaspoons baking soda dissolved in a little water


2 teaspoons Anise extract or Anise oil


1 cup chopped cashews or peanuts or pecans or almonds or

any type of nut/seed that you desire.


4 1/2 to 5 cups of flour
