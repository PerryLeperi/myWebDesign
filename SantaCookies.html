Santa Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.


In a large bowl, cream butters and 3/4 cup sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and extracts. Combine the flour,


baking powder and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture and mix well.


Divide dough into thirds. Stir red food coloring into 1 portion of dough; stir green food coloring into another portion.


Leave remaining dough plain. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.


Divide each portion of dough into 4 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a 12-in. rope. Place a red, a green and a plain


rope next to each other. Braid one color of each together. Cut through all 3 ropes at 1-inch intervals, forming sets of


3 differently colored doughs. Repeat.


Roll each set of doughs into a ball; place balls 3 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets. Flatten to 1/8-inch thickness


with a glass dipped in remaining sugar. Bake at 375Â° for 8-10 minutes or until bottoms are lightly browned. Remove to


wire racks to cool.


1 cup butter, softened


3/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon granulated sugar divided


1 large egg, room temperature


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract


2 cup all purpose flour


1/2 teaspoon baking powder


Pinch of salt


10-20 drops of red food coloring


10-20 drops of green food coloring
