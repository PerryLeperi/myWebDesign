Grandmother's Cake

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.


Grease an 8 or 9 inch round cake pan.


Using a microwave safe bowl, melt chocolate and 1/2 of the butter. Mix until chocolate is melted and smooth.


Add in additional butter pieces.


Add egg white and yokes one at a time.


Blending well after each addition. Stir in the sugar and pour into cake pan, smoothing the top.


Bake for 25 minutes. Cake will be very soft in the center. Let cool to room temperature. Refrigerate overnight.


Garnish with powdered sugar and walnuts.


10 ounces semi-sweet chocolate chips (1 bag)


2 sticks butter, cut into tablespoon portions


3 egg whites


5 egg yokes


1/2 cup granulated sugar


Powdered sugar (garnish)


Walnut halves (garnish)
