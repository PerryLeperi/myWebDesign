Grandpa's Chocolate Cheesecake

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.


To make the crust: crush, grind or otherwise pulverize the cookies together with sugar. Set aside about 2 tablespoons


of cookie crumbs for garnish.


In a food processor add crushed cookies and peanut butter. Mix until thoroughly combined.


Line the bottom of a springform cake pan. Pour cookie crust contents into the pan and spread evenly. Bake the crust for


about 15 minutes.


Reduce heat to 350 degrees.


To make the filling: Combine the milk and chocolate chips in a small microwave safe mixing bowl. Heat in the microwave for


about 2 minutes. Mix until chocolate is thoroughly melted. Add in coffee crystals and set mixture aside.


In a large mixing bowl, beat together cream cheese, sugar and extract. Beat until creamy smooth. Scrape down the sides of


the bowl. On low to medium speed, add in one egg at a time until thoroughly combined. Then add in flour. Add in chocolate


mixture slowly until completely combined. Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl. Mix until smooth.


Pour batter atop the crust in the pan. Place the pan on a baking sheet (this will help get the cake in and out of the oven


safely).


Bake the cake for 45 - 50 minutes at 350 degrees. To check for doneness, insert a toothpick into the cake about 1 inch from


the edge of the cake pan. Toothpick should come out clean. If you use a thermometer, it should read 175 degrees. The center


may not look set, that's ok. Turn off the oven and let the cake sit in the oven with the oven door ajar for about an hour.


Once the cake has cooled, garnish and refrigerate until ready to serve.


As an extra bonus, I like to also garnish with fresh raspberries or freshly cut strawberries.


Crust:


24 chocolate sandwich cookies (Oreos)


1/4 cup powdered sugar


1/3 cup peanut butter


Filling:


1/2 cup whole milk


2 cups semisweet chocolate chips


2 tablespoons instant coffee crystals or freeze dried coffee.


3 8 ounce packages cream cheese (room temperature)


1 cup granulated sugar


4 large eggs


1 tablespoon rum extract


2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
