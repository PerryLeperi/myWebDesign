Grandpa's Parfait

Instructions

Ingredients

Base

Preheat oven to 400 degrees


Mix butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until combined. Stir in flour, nuts, and salt until


dough holds together. Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thickness, With round cookie cutter, cut out as many


cookies possible. Place cookies on cookie sheet approximately 1 inch apart.


Bake for about 10 minutes or until set but not brown. Remove from the cookie sheet once cookies have cooled down.


Parfait Base

Using a round mold ( approximately the same size used for cookie base) height should be no more than 2 inches.


Cut the vanilla bean lengthwise with a small sharp knife. Scrape and collect the black seeds. Pour milk in sauce pan,


adding vanilla seeds. Heat milk and vanilla—do not allow it to boil. Remove from heat and cover.


In a mixing bowl, add sugar and eggs. Whisk them for about 1 minute. Add in vanilla bean milk, combine briskly.


Pour this preparation back into saucepan. Heat at low heat, stirring constantly—like making custard. DO NOT LET


MILK BOIL!


Once thickened, remove from heat. Remove vanilla beans. Pour vanilla milk into mixing bowl and mix in high for 5 to 6


minutes, then at medium speed for 10 minutes.


Beat the cold cream firmly. Delicately, add and combine the Amaretto, cream and vanilla contents.


Fill mold (about 2 inch thickness) with parfait liquid base. Place molds in freezer for about 5 - 6 hours until frozen solid.


Plate on Dessert plate. Top Cookie with Parfait base.


Sauce & Toppings:

In a bowl add Milk and chocolate. Microwave for about 2 minutes. Mix until combined. Drizzle hot sauce over Parfait Base.


Sprinkle with brittle & crushed Almonds. Add 1 whole Almond to top center


Base:

1 cup butter (softened)


1/2 cup powdered sugar


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour


1 cup finely chopped almonds


1/2 cup Powdered Sugar


Parfait Base:

2 vanilla beans


2 cups whole milk


3 cups powdered sugar


6 eggs


2 cups cold cream


1/2 cup Amaretto


Sauce & Toppings:

4 ounces milk chocolate


1/4 cup crushed nut brittle


1/2 cup whole Milk


1/4 cup crushed Almonds


Whole Almonds (garnish)
