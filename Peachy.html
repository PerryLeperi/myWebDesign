Peachy Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

In a separate mixing bowl add chocolates, coffee, milk. Microwave for about 1 - 1/2 minutes. Whisk together until


thoroughly mixed, let cool. Over medium heat, warm the peaches, cinnamon, honey, water and cayenne pepper until it


starts to boil. Strain peach mixture, saving the liquid portion.


In a cold sauce pan, crack eggs into pan. Scoop about 1 cup of whipped cream into the sauce pan. Over low heat warm the


egg and peach combination. Using a hand whisk, constantly whisk. Keep whisking until the batter becomes like a custard


consistency. Slowly add in the whipped cream. Slowly add the peach sauce. Combine thoroughly.


*To cut time, add the cut up peaches, cinnamon, honey and water to smoothie machine or juicer.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup granulated Sugar


1 teaspoon Vanilla extract


4 peaches cut up


1 teaspoon cinnamon


1/4 cup honey


1 cup water


3 large eggs


1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
