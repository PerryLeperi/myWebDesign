Chocolate Eclairs

Instructions

Ingredients

Puffs:

Preheat oven at 400 degrees


Heat water and butter in a saucepan to the boiling point. Stir in flour. Stir constantly until mixture leaves the pan and

forms into a ball (about 1 minute). Remove from heat.


One at a time, beat and add in 4 eggs. Beat until mixture is smooth and velvety.


Drop from spoon onto an ungreased baking sheet. Batch should make 16 medium size puffs. Bake for about 45 to 50 minutes until

dry. Allow to cool slowly.


Custard Filling:

Mix together in a saucepan over low heat, sugar, salt flour and milk. Stir until it starts to boil. Stir and fold for about

1 minute and remove from heat.


Stir mixture into 2 beaten eggs and blend in vanilla extract. Let cool.


Fill Puffs with custard filling.


Chocolate Icing

Melt together butter and chocolate.


Blend in powdered sugar and water.


Beat until smooth but not stiff. Drizzle over Eclair Puffs. Let them cool for about 3 minutes before devouring them.


Puffs:

1 cup water


1/2 cup butter


4 eggs


1 cup flour


Custard Filling:

1/2 cup granulated sugar


1/2 teaspoon salt


6 tablespoons all-purpose flour


2 cups milk


2 eggs


2 teaspoons vanilla extract


Chocolate Icing


2 tablespoons butter


2 ounces baker's chocolate


1 cup sifted powdered sugar


2 tablespoons boiling water
