Candy Cane S'mores

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 300 degrees.


In a microwave safe bowl, place the chocolate chips and heavy cream and heat until melted and smooth, stirring every


30 seconds.


Line the graham crackers on a cookie sheet, top with marshmallows and place in oven for about 2 minutes or until the


marshmallows are slightly golden on top and puffed.


As soo as the pan of marshmallows is out of the oven, pour melted chocolate in a pastry bag, cut the tip with the scissors


(small hole) and pipe on the toasted marshmallows.


Sprinkle cand cane pieces on top and enjoy with a nice hot cup hot chocolate.


16 graham crackers (8 halved)


16 big marshmallows


1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips


5 tablespoons heavy whipping cream


1 cup crushed candy canes
