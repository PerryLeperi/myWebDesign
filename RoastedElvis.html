Roasted Elvis Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 400 degrees.


Place banana slices, brown sugar, and butter in a saucepan. Place in the oven. After 15 minutes, stir and place


back in the oven for another 15 minutes. Watch so sugar doesn't burn. Stir again. Pour banana mixture into a food


processor, add in Peanut or Almond Butter and milk. Puree. Let cool to room temperature.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer


speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in banana combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


5 ripe bananas cut into 1 inch thick slices (cut the long way)


1/2 cup brown sugar


1 cup creamy Peanut Butter or Almond Butter


2 tablespoons butter


1 cup milk
