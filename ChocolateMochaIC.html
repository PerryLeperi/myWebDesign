Chocolate Mocha Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients


In a small mixing bowl add instant coffee, milk and Kahlua. Mix until coffee crystals dissolve.


Add chocolates to coffee combination. Microwave for about 1 - 1/2 minutes.


Whisk together until thoroughly mixed, let cool to room temperature.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the


mixer speed to high for about 5 minutes. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Slowly add the


chocolate/coffee mixture to the whipping cream and combine thoroughly.


Pour into ice cream mixing churn and Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.



1/2 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1 cup granulated Sugar


1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract


1/4 cup Dark chocolate chips


1/4 cup Milk chocolate chips


1 cup milk


1/2 cup Kahlua (optional)


1/3 cup instant freeze dried coffee
