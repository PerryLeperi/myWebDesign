Butterscotch Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients



In a small mixing bowl add butterscotch chips and milk. Heat in microwave for about 1 1/2 minutes.

Mix thoroughly.

Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase

the mixer speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in butterscotch combination

and mix thoroughly.


Pour into ice cream mixing churn and make according to the ice cream maker's instructions.


1/2 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1 cup butterscotch chips


1 cup milk
