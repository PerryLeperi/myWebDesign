Banana Espresso Nib Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

Adjust oven rack to the middle of the oven. Preheat to 350 degrees. Line 3 cookie sheets with parchment paper.


In mixing bowl with paddle, on slow medium speed add ripe banana and butter. Mix for about 2 minutes until creamy. Add in


sugars, vanilla, almond flour, egg, water baking soda. Increase to medium speed until combined thoroughly. (About 3 minutes)


Turn mixer speed to slow. Add 1/4 cup all-purpose flour at a time until combined.


Turn off mixer and scrape down the sides of the mixing bowl.


Add in nibs and semisweet/bittersweet chocolate. On slow speed mix until combined.


Using a tablespoon, Spoon out a dollop (about 2 ounces) and place on a cookie sheet. The dough will spread so place about


3 inches apart.


Place the cookie sheet in the oven and bake for about 15 minutes. Let cookies cool on the pan before transferring them.


Makes about 24 cookies


1 Ripe Banana


1 1/2 sticks butter (at room temperature)


1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour


1/2 cup almond flour


2 Tablespoon ground espresso


1 1/4 cup granulated sugar


1/4 cup brown sugar


1 large egg


1 tablespoon water
