Bananas Foster

Instructions

Ingredients

Using the stand mixer, add heavy cream, granulated sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
Mix thoroughly. Slowly increase mixer speed to high for about 5 minutes. Pour cream batter into ice
cream machine. Make according to ice cream makers instructions.

Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
Add bananas and stir for about 2 minutes. Add brown sugar, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, cinnamon and salt.
Lower heat and continue cooking, stirring constantly for about 2 minutes. Optional to add rum.
Gently spoon Bananas Foster over freshly made ice cream.

  • 2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream
    1 cup granulated sugar
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    2 tablespoons butter
    3 bananas sliced
    1 cup brown sugar
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    Pinch of salt
    1/4 cup rum (optional)
