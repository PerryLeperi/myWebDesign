Blueberry Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients


In a medium-sized sauce pan, heat up the blueberries, orange juice, lemon juice,
and 1/2 cup sugar over medium heat until sugar dissolves.
Bring to a slight boil.

Strain berry juice into a bowl. Using a spoon, press all the juice from the berries.
Pour juice back into the sauce pan and let cool.

In a medium size bowl, whisk the eggs and half-and-half. Add in 1 cup of berry juice
to the half-and-half. Mix thoroughly. Add half-and-half mixture to the berry juice,
continue whisking. Over medium heat add in remaining half-and-half to berry juice.
Continue whisking until batter becomes thicker, almost custard density. Remove from
heat and let cool for about 30 minutes.

In the stand mixer, using the whisk, whip up the heavy whipping cream, vanilla,
and 1/2 cup sugar until light and fluffy.
Using a 1/2 cup measuring cup, transfer berry juice to cream. Wait about 30 seconds
before adding additional juice.

Pour ice cream batter into Ice Cream Machine and make ice cream according to Ice Cream
machine's instructions.



1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream

1 cup Half-and-Half

1 cup granulated sugar, divided in half

2 cups blueberries

3 large eggs

1/2 cup orange juice

1 teaspoon vanilla
