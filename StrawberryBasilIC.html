Strawberry Basil Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Blitz 1 cup freeze-dried strawberries in a food processor until a dust forms.


In a medium-sized sauce pan, whisk together strawberry dust, egg yokes, sugar and salt over medium heat until


sugar dissolves.


Whisk in milk and cream until combined. Add in 1/4 cup basil leaves.


Bring heat to a medium-high and bring to a simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature,


about 1 hour.


Strain mixture through a metal sieve into a large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and chill at least 4 hours or


overnight.


Pour ice cream batter into Ice Cream Machine and make ice cream according to Ice Cream machine's instructions.


About 7 minutes before the ice cream is done crush the remaining 1/2 cup freeze-dried strawberries and add to the


ice cream machine along with 1/4 cup basilâ€”thinly sliced. Stir until combined.


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


3/4 cup granulated sugar


1 1/2 cups freeze dried strawberries


6 egg yokes


1 teaspoon salt


1 cup Whole milk


1/2 cup fresh basil
