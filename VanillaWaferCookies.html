Vanilla Wafer Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 350 and line baking pans with parchment paper.


Using the stand mixer, cream the butter on high for about 2 minutes.


Turn mixer speed to medium, add in sugar and continue to mix until mixture is light and fluffy and there are no more lumps,


about 2 minutes.


Add in eggs and mix until smooth, Add in the vanilla. (If the mix curdles don't worry, just give it a good whisk at bring


it all back together.)


Add in the flour and salt, mmix until it just comes together. Do not overmix. The batter will be somewhat soft.


Scrape down the sides of the bowl in order to place batter in a piping bag fitted with a medium pastry tip. Pipe it in even


mounds onto the baking pan (about 2 teaspoons). Keep cookies about 1 1/2 inches apart as batter will spread when baked.


Bake for about 18 to 20 minutes or until golden.


1 stick (1/2 cup) butter, softened


1 2/3 cup powdered sugar, sifted


3 large eggs, room temperature


2 teaspoons vanilla extract


1 1/3 cups sifted all-purpose flour


A pinch of salt
