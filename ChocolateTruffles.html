Chocolate Truffles

Instructions

Ingredients

Place the chocolate ina heat-proof bowl. Set aside.


Heat the heavy whipping cream until it is simmering. You can heat it on the stove or in the microwave.


Add the butter and heavy whipping cream. Let the warm cream and chocolate sit for about 5 minutes. Add vanilla then


stir until the chocolate has completely melted.


Pour chocolate combination into an 8 x 8 inch baking dish, so the mixture evenly and quickly sets. Place a piece of


plastic wrap directly on the surface of the chocolate to avoid condensation. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours.


Scoop the set truffle mixture into 2 teaspoon-sized mounds. When rolling truffle between your pals, size should be about


1 inch in diameter.


This gets a little sticky, (so depending on the desired topping) roll in dry desired topping.


If rolling in chocolate, place truffle on lined cookie sheet and place in freezer for an hour before dipping in chocolate.


8 ounces baker's chocolate, chopped finely


2/3 cup Heavy Whipping Cream


1 tablespoon butter


1//2 teaspoon vanilla extract


Toppings: Your choice - unsweetened cocoa powder, Sprinkles, Crushed nuts, or Melted *chocolate - dark or milk chocolate


*If using a chocolate topping, combine 2 cups chocolate and 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream.
