Chocolate Raspberry Truffles

Instructions

Ingredients

Place the chocolate chips and whipping cream into a microwave safe bowl. Microwave 20 seconds at a time, mixing


between heating intervals. Mix until all the chocolate is melted and blended smoothly.


Blend the freeze dried raspberries until they're crushed, then add them into the melted chocolate until fully combined.


Pour chocolate combination into an 8 x 8 inch baking dish, so the mixture evenly and quickly sets. Place a piece of


plastic wrap directly on the surface of the chocolate to avoid condensation. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours.


Scoop the set truffle mixture into 2 teaspoon-sized mounds. When rolling truffle between your pals, size should be


about 1 inch in diameter. Place on lined baking sheets, ensuring truffles don't touch each other.


Melt the chocolate chips until smooth. Dip each truffle into the melted chocolate and sprinkle with remaining crushed


raspberries.


Store truffles in the refrigerator until you're ready to serve them


2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips


1/4 cup Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup freeze dried raspberries


For the Coating:


1 cup chocolate chips


3 tblsp freeze dried raspberries
