Kahlua & Amaretto Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, and vanilla. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer


speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy.


Add in Liquors. Combine thoroughly. Make ice cream according to Ice Cream Maker's instructions.


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1/3 cup Kahlua Liquor


1/3 cup Amaretto Liquor
