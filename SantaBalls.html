Santa's Balls

Instructions

Ingredients

Cut open chia tea bags and pour into bowl. (If contents are whole and not powdered, grind the chia tea to powder.)


Place 1 cup of shredded coconut with the chia tea, mix well.


Add the honey, coconut oil, 3/4 cup peppermint, and salt and blend until well combined. Add remaining shredded coconut


and peppermint, mix until thoroughly combined.


Shape into 12 (1-inch) balls with wet hands. Freeze for at least an hour to so.


Santa Balls are enjoyed cold or at room temperature.


1 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut


2 Chia tea bags


1/8 cup honey


1 Tbsp melted coconut oil


1 cup candy canes (crushed)


1/8 tsp sea salt
