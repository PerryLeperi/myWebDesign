Mississippi Mud Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

1. In a medium-sized saucepan, over medium heat, combine heavy whipping cream, milk, sugar, cocoa powder, coffee, and salt.


2. Heat until the cocoa powder has dissolved and the mixture is just simmering.


3. Add the chocolate to the saucepan. Stir until completely melted.


4. Pour mixture into a large bowl.


5. Stir in vanilla.


6. Press plastic wrap to the surface of the mixture. Chill in the refrigerator for 3 hours, or until cold.


7. Churn the ice cream batter in the bowl of your ice cream machine according to your manufacturer's instructions.


8. In the last 2 minutes, add 90% of the cookie crumbs, churn until done.


9. Spoon half of the ice cream into a shallow container or loaf pan. Add half of the fudge sauce and lightly swirl with


a spoon.


10. Repeat step 9 creating a second layer. Sprinkle the remaining cook crumbs over the top.


11. Press plastic wrap or wax paper to the surface of the ice cream. Freeze for at least 4 hours.


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 cup whole milk


3/4 cup granulated sugar


1/4 cup cocoa powder


2 tablespoons instant coffee crystals


1/4 teaspoon salt


4 oz semisweet chocolate, chopped


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1 cup chocolate fudge sauce


2 cups Oreo sandwich cookies, roughly chopped
