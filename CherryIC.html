Cherry Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients


Over medium heat, warm the cherries, water and food coloring until it starts to boil.


Strain cherry mixture, saving both the liquid portion and fruit portion. Cut and pit cherries.


Save the cut cherries. Let cool to room temperature.


In a cold sauce pan, crack eggs into pan. Add cherry liquid into the sauce pan. Over low heat


warm the egg and cherry combination. Using a hand whisk, constantly whisk. Keep whisking until


the batter becomes like a custard consistency. Slowly add in the whipped cream. Slowly add the


cherry sauce. Combine thoroughly.


*To cut time, add the cut up cherries, food coloring and water to smoothie machine or juicer.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer


speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in cherry combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Add in cherry chunks during the last 2 minutes of churning.



1/2 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


2 cups pitted and cut cherries


1 cup water


3 large eggs


5 - 6 red food coloring drops
