Fruit Festival Sorbet

Instructions

Ingredients

Place all ingredients into a large bowl, mix until sugar dissolves. Place in the refrigerator for about 2 hours. Pour


into the food processor until pureed. Transfer into Ice Cream Maker and freeze according to Ice Cream Maker's instructions.


1 cup blueberries cut


1 cup blackberries cut


1 cup strawberries cut


2 red pears cut/diced


2 mandarin oranges squeezed


2 lemons squeezed


1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper


1 teaspoon cinnamon


1/2 cup granulated sugar


2 tablespoons corn syrup


2 1/2 cups cold water
