Peanut Butter Balls

Instructions

Ingredients

Line the baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.


Mix peanut butter, butter, 1 cup chopped nuts and vanilla together in a large mixing bowl.


Slowly add one cup of powdered sugar at a time, mixing thoroughly until combined. By the time you get to the third


cup of powdered sugar, the mixture will become very difficult to stir. Make sure your hands are clean and take off any


hand or wrist jewelry. Mix the remaining by using your hands.


Place remaining chopped peanuts in a bowl.


Once fully incorporated, pinch off enough to make 1 inch ball and roll in your hands forming a ball. Place peanut


butter balls on a lined baking sheet. Freeze for about 1 hour.


One at a time, dip each peanut butter ball into melted chocolate, then roll each chocolate covered Peanut Butter ball


in crushed peanuts coating the entire ball. Set on a clean lined cookie sheet to cool and harden. This can get very


messy, but it's well worth it!!!


1 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter


1/2 cup butter (softened)


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


3 cups powdered sugar


4 cups chopped peanuts (divided)


12 ounces Chocolate Chips for dipping
