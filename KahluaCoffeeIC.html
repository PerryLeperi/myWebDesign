Kahlua & Coffee Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

In a smaller mixing bowl, combine Liquor, coffee crystals and milk until coffee dissolves.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, and vanilla. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy.


Add in coffee combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour into ice cream mixing churn and make according to the ice cream maker's instructions.


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


3/4 cup Kahlua Liquor


1/3 cup Instant Coffee Crystals


1 cup whole milk
