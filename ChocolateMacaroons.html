Chocolate Macaroons

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.


Arrange wire rack in the middle of oven (horizontally, not vertically).


Fill a small saucepan 1/3 of the way full with water, and place a small bowl on top, making sure the bottom of the bowl is not


touching the water. Place chocolate chips in the bowl. Heat over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally until the ingredients.


chocolate is melted.


Remove the saucepan from heat. Let the chocolate cool until just warm to the touch. Meanwhile prepare the remaining


Place coconut in food processor with blade attachment and process until fluffy and snow like, about 1 minute.


Add the melted chocolate and vanilla to the processor until combined, scraping down the sides as needed.


Scoop level tablespoonfuls of the dough onto the baking sheets, spacing them at least 1 inch apart. The cookies will not


spread, but they do puff slightly.


Bake until cookies are shiny and a bit stringy when squeezed, about 20 minutes. Let cool for about 10 minutes. Dust with


powdered sugar before serving.


2/3 cup bittersweet chocolate chips


4 1/2 cups sweetened shredded coconut


1/2 cup granulated sugar


3 large egg whites


3/8 cup cocoa powder


Pinch of salt


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


Powdered sugar for dusting
