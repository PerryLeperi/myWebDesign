Grandpa's Ice Cream Sandwich

Instructions

Ingredients


Sandwich ends:

In a medium bowl, cream together the chocolate chips, peanut butter, cream cheese, sugars, and


vanilla with an electric mixer until well combined. Add eggs one at a time, mixing in between.


In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, salt, and baking soda. Add to wet ingredients and mix


on low until combined.


Roll out 1/4 inch thickness on parchment paper. Cut to desired uniform sandwich size. Place on cookie


sheets and place in the refrigerator. Let dough chill for at least one hour, up to 2 days.


When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Bake for 6 - 8 minutes.


Let cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to cool on a wire rack.


Filling:


In a mixing bowl, add milk, chocolate, cinnamon and cayenne pepper. Heat in microwave for about 1 1/2 minutes.


Mix thoroughly let cool.


In a mixing bowl, add whipping cream, sugar, and vanilla. Whip until light and frothy.


Add chocolate to ice cream base and mix thoroughly.


Add ice cream batter to your ice cream machine, churn until done.


Place ice cream in the freezer for an hour or two to firm up a little.


Assembling the Sandwich:


With a butter knife or spatula, spread 1/2 inch ice cream over a cookie, top with another cookie. Makes


about 30 sandwich cookies.


This assembly will be quick if you're organized. I have saved some time and steps by pouring the ice cream


into a decorating sleeve before placing it in the freezer to form up.


Assemble all the top and bottom sandwich ends in a row next to each other. I prefer to use a fat wide tip on


the sleeve. Depending on how thick you want your ice cream sandwiches, it will be easier to regulate the


consistency of the batter. Squeeze out the desired thickness over the bottom cookies, place tops on and place


in the freezer for about 3 hours. Wrap in parchment paper, wax paper, or your own wrappers and save until desired.



Sandwich Ends:

1 cup (8 oz) full fat cream


1 cup creamy peanut butter (Creamy)


3/4 cup brown sugar


1/4 cup granulated sugar


1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract


2 eggs - room temperature


2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour


1 teaspoon kosher salt


1 teaspoon baking soda


1 cup mini chocolate chips


Filling


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 cup granulated sugar


1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract


1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon


1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper


1 teaspoon sea salt


1 cup dark chocolate


1 cup whole milk
