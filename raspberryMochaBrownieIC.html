Raspberry Mocha Brownie Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Spread out the remaining pint of raspberries over parchment paper,


ensuring no raspberries touch one another. Place in freezer.


Using 2 pints of raspberries with water. Heat over medium heat, bring to a slight boil. Add in freeze dried coffee


and mix thoroughly. Do not strain. Let cool to room temperature.


In a cold sauce pan, crack eggs into pan. Add raspberry liquid into the sauce pan. Over low heat warm the egg and


raspberry combination. Using a hand whisk, constantly whisk. Keep whisking until the batter becomes like a custard


consistency. Slowly add to the whipped cream. Slowly add the raspberry sauce. Lastly, add in food coloring. Combine


thoroughly.


Mix freeze dried coffee with milk until coffee is dissolved. Add to Ice Cream base and mix thoroughly.


Add Brownie chunks and frozen raspberries into churned Ice cream 2 minutes before the end of the Ice Cream Churning


is done.


Make ice cream according to Ice Cream Maker's instructions. Once Ice Cream is done, pour Ice Cream into a large bowl,


fold in brownie crumbles and remaining frozen raspberries.


1 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1/2 cup water


5 - 6 drops red food coloring


1/2 cup milk


1/2 cup water


3 large eggs


1/2 cup freeze dried coffee


3 pints raspberries


2 cups crumbled brownies
