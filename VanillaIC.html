Grandpa's Vanilla Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

Put all ingredients into the mixing bowl. Whisk whip in stand mixer for about 5 minutes gradually increase mixer speed


from slow to fast within the first 5 minutes. Add to your mixing churn and make according to the ice cream maker's


instructions.


*Notes*

The fun part of this recipe is there are countless assorted flavors which you can add except fruit. Also if you add


1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon you will have a French Vanilla ice cream.



1/2 cup granulated sugar


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
