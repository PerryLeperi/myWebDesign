Nut'n for you my Sweetness

Instructions

Ingredients

Part 1: Mocha Ice Cream:


Add coffee crystals to milk. Stir until dissolved.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in coffee combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Part 2: Peanut Butter Ice Cream


In a microwave safe bowl, add milk and peanut butter. Heat in the microwave for about 1 and a half minutes. Add rum. Whisk


together until thoroughly combined.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually increase the mixer speed


to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in peanut butter combination and mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Once the peanut butter portion is done, scoop over the Mocha Ice Cream waiting in the freezer. Fold Ice Cream until you get


a good marbling look.


Part 1: Mocha Ice Cream


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup granulated sugar


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1 cup milk


2 tablespoons instant coffee crystals


Part 2: Peanut Butter Ice Cream


2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1 cup granulated sugar


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1 cup chopped peanuts


1 cup Peanut Butter


1/2 cup Spiced Rum


1/2 cup milk
