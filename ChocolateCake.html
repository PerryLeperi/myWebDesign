Chocolate Cake

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.


Prepare a round cake pan with butter, then flour.


Melt chocolate chips with half of the butter. Use a microwave safe bowl. Microwave 1 minute at a time. Mix butter and


chocolate well -reheat in microwave 1 minute at a time adding more butter each time. Repeat mixing process until all


butter and chocolate is melted.


Add one egg at a time and mix well before adding additional eggs. Stir in sugar.


Pour batter into cake pan, smoothing the top.


Bake for 25 minutes. Cake will be very soft in the center. Cool to room temperature. Place cake on a serving platter. Garnish


with powdered sugar, crushed walnuts and refrigerate overnight.


10 ounces semi sweet chocolate chips (1 bag)


2 sticks butter - cut into tablespoon sizes let warm to room temperature.


4 large eggs


1/2 cup granulated sugar


Powdered sugar for garnishing


Chopped walnuts for garnishing
