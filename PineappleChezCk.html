Pineapple Cheese Cake

Instructions

Ingredients

Mix crumbs and butter. Press onto bottom and sides of a buttered 9-inch spring-form pan.


Drain pineapple, reserve syrup. Add gelatin to pineapple syrup. Mix well. In a double boiler combine egg yolks,


sugar, lemon peel, salt and 2 tablespoons pineapple syrup.


Cook and stir over hot, not boiling water 5 to 8 minutes or until like smooth custard (mixture will seem to get thin


as it cooks).


Remove from heat; add the remaining pineapple syrup mixture and stir until dissolved. Add crushed pineapple, cottage


cheese, and vanilla. Fold in whipped cream, then the egg whites.


Pour into crumb lined pan. Spread evenly.


Chill for 5 hours, or until firm.


In removing cake from pan, first loosen sides with spatula.


2 1/3 cups vanilla wafer crumbs


1/2 cup butter. Melted


1 can of crushed pineapple


1 1.75 ounce box of unflavored gelatin


1 cup grated lemon peel


slightly beaten egg yolks


1 cup granulated sugar


Pinch of salt


3 cups small-curd cream-style cottage cheese


1 tablespoon vanilla extract


2 cups heavy whipping cream


4 stiff-beaten egg whites
