Caramel Sauce

Instructions

Ingredients

Combine sugar, corn syrup, and water in a saucepan and stir.


Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring gently and frequently to ensure that the sugar dissolves.


When the mixture begins to simmer and the side are completely washed down, stop stirring and insert a candy thermometer.


Cook until mixture reaches 360 degrees Freiheit.


Remove from heat and slowly stir in the cream. Be cautious as the mixture will splatter. Stir in the vanilla extract and


butter, adding a couple tablespoons at a time until it is all melted and mixed. Cool caramel sauce completely before storing.


2 cups granulated sugar


2 tablespoon light corn syrup


1/2 cup water


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


3/4 cup heavy whipping cream


1/2 cup butter, cut into pieces
