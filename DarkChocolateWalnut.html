Dark Chocolate Walnut Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients


In a medium-sized sauce pan, whisk together molasses, sugar and salt over medium


heat until sugar dissolves.


Whisk in milk and cream until combined.


Bring heat to a medium-high and bring to a simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let


cool to room temperature, about 1 hour.


Transfer mixture to a large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and chill at least 4 hours or overnight.


Pour ice cream batter into Ice Cream Machine and make ice cream according to Ice Cream machine's


instructions


About 2 minutes before the ice cream is done, add in walnut and chocolate to the batch.


Churn until combined.



2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream


1/2 cup granulated sugar


1/4 cup molasses


1 teaspoon salt


1 cup Whole milk


1 tablespoon vanilla extract


1 teaspoon salt


1/2 cup walnut pieces


3 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
