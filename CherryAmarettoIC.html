Cherry Amaretto Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla and sugar. Start mixing on low, gradually


increase the mixer speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy. Add in cherry juice


from jar and amaretto, mix thoroughly.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


Add in cherry chunks during the last 2 minutes of churning.



1 cup granulated sugar


1 cup granulated sugar


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


1 10 oz jar maraschino cherries cut in half and stems removed


1/2 cup amaretto liqueur
