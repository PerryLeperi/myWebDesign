Coconut Rum Balls

Instructions

Ingredients

In a large bowl mix crushed cookies, 1 1/3 cups coconut and nuts.


Stir in sweetened condensed milk and rum.


Mix well.


Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours.


Roll dough into 1 inch balls


Roll Rum Balls in coconut flakes.


Store and refrigerate rum balls in tightly covered container for about 2 days for best taste.


12 ounce package vanilla crackers


1 1/3 cups flaked coconut


1 cup chopped nuts (pecan, almonds or walnuts)


14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk


1/4 cup rum


1 cup coconut flakes
