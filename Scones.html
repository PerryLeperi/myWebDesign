Scones

Instructions

Ingredients

Whisk together flour, salt and baking soda in a large bowl. Grate frozen butter using a grater or grate with a food processor.


Add grated butter to the flour mixture. Combine with a pastry cutter, two forks or your fingers. Mix until it becomes pea sized


crumbs.


In a small mixing bowl, whisk together heavy cream, egg and vanilla extract. Drizzle over the flour mixture and combine your


add-ins.


Mix until everything is moistened.


Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.


I don't make triangle scones as it does get to be such a process as the dough is very sticky. I have found it easier just


to take a teaspoon and scoop out a portion and drop it on the cookie sheet about 2 - 3 inches apart.


Depending on the oven and the size of scones, the time will be longer if the scones are bigger.


Generally I drop about 2 inch dough globs on the cookie sheet 2 -3 inches apart. Bake for about 15 to 18 minutes.


In a coffee cup, combine the topping ingredients and mix thoroughly.


Once scones are baked, brush the sugar coating over the top of each scone. Move scones to the cooling rack.


2 cups all-purpose flour


1/2 cup granulated sugar


1/2 teaspoon salt


2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder


1/2 cup unsalted butter (1 stick {frozen})


1/2 cup heavy cream or buttermilk


1 large egg


1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract


1 1/2 cups Add-ins such as chocolate chips, berries, nuts, fruit, etc.


For topping:


1/2 cup water


1/3 cup powdered sugar
