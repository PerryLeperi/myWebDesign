Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

In a medium size bowl, cream together chocolate chips, peanut butter, cream cheese, sugars and vanilla extract with


a mixer. Add in an egg one at a time, mixing thoroughly between each egg added.


In a separate bowl, mix together flour, salt and baking soda. Once mixed, little by little, add flour combination with


peanut butter combination. Mix until all is combined.


Let dough chill in the refrigerator for at least one hour (up to 2 days).


When ready, preheat the oven to 350 degrees.


Scoop 2 tablespoon sized balls onto cookie sheets. Flatten balls with the palm of your hand.


Bake for about 8 - 10 minutes.


Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.


1 cup (8 ounces) cream cheese (softened)


1 cup peanut butter


3/4 cup brown sugar (packed)


1/4 cup granulated sugar


1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract


2 Large Eggs (room temperature)


2 1/4 cups sifted all-purpose flour
