Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Instructions

Ingredients

In a saucepan over medium heat, warm the mint leaves and milk for about 5 minutes. Strain out the leaves


and set aside. Let cool to room temperature.


Using a stand mixer, combine whipping cream, vanilla, and honey. Start mixing on low, gradually increase


the mixer speed to high. Whisk the whipping cream until light and fluffy.


Slowly incorporate the mint milk with the Ice Cream base.


Pour batter into the churn of your Ice Cream Maker. Make according to ice cream maker's instructions.


During the last 2 minutes of churning, add chocolate.


2 cups heavy whipping cream


1 tablespoon of vanilla extract


3/4 cup honey


2 cups fresh mint leaves


2 cups whole milk


1 cup chopped/shaved dark chocolate
