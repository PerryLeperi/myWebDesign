Toasted Sesame Seed Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

Adjust oven rack to the middle of the oven. Preheat to 350 degrees. Line 3 cookie sheets with parchment paper.


In a small bowl mix together flour, salt and baking soda.


In the bowl of the mixer, fitted with paddle, beat butter on medium speed until creamy - about 45 seconds to a minute.


Add in sugars and beat on medium speed until light and fluffy, around 2 minutes. Add egg, sesame oil, water, and vanilla.


Mix on low speed until thoroughly combined, add in the chocolate chips and mix until combined.


Pour sesame seeds in a bowl.


Using a tablespoon, Spoon out a dollop (about 2 ounces) and drop in sesame seeds and cover the dollop evenly. Place


sesame seed dollop on a cookie sheet. The dough will spread so place about 3 inches apart.


Place the cookie sheet in the oven and bake for about 13 minutes. Let cookies cool on the pan before transferring them.


Makes about 16 cookies


1 3/4 cups all-purpose Flour


3/4 teaspoon salt


1/2 teaspoon baking soda


1 1/2 sticks (12 tablespoons) butter - room temperature


1 cup granulated sugar


1/2 cup brown sugar


1 large egg


2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil


1 tablespoon water
