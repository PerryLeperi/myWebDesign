Rustic Nut Bars

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 375. Line a 13 x 9 baking pan with foil, letting ends extend over the sides by at least 1 inch.


Grease foil with 1 tablespoon butter.


In a large bowl, whisk flour, sugar and salt. Cut in the remaining butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.


Stir in egg until well blended (mixture will be dry). Press firmly onto the bottom of prepared pan.


Bake for about 18 to 20 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. Cool on wire rack.


In a large saucepan, combine honey, brown sugar, and salt; bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring frequently


to dissolve sugar. Boil for about 2 minutes without stirring. Stir in butter and cream; return to a boil. Cook and


stir 1 minute or until smooth. Remove from heat; stir in nuts and spread over crust.


Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until topping is bubbly. Cool completely in pan on a wire rack. Lifting with foil, remove from pan.


Discard foil; cut into bars.


3/4 cup cold butter plus 1 tablespoon of butter


2 1/3 cup all purpose flour


1/2 cup granulated sugar


1/2 teaspoon baking powder


1/2 teaspoon salt


1 large egg slightly beaten at room temperature


2/3 cup honey


1/2 cup packed brown sugar


6 tablespoons butter, cubed


2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream


1 cup hazelnuts, toasted


1 cup salted cashews


1 cup pistachios


1 cup salted roasted almonds
