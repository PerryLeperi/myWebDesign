Butterscotch Fudge

Instructions

Ingredients

In a heavy saucepan, combine sugar, butter, and evaporated milk. Stir over medium heat until boiling, then mix in


butterscotch chips, marshmallow crème, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.


Quickly pour into a greased 9-inch square pan, spread evenly.


Allow to cool at room temperature. Cut into squares before serving.


2 cups granulated sugar


3/4 cup butter


2/3 cup evaporated milk


1 package (10 oz) butterscotch chips


1 jar (7 oz) marshmallow crème


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1/4 teaspoon salt
