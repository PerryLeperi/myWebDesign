Dreamsicle Cookies

Instructions

Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees - make sure the oven rack is on the middle level as you will only bake one cookie sheet


at a time.


Line 3 cookie sheets with parchment paper


In a medium mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda.


In the bowl of the mixer with a paddle, beat the butter on medium until creamy - about 45 seconds, Add sugar and


orange zest until light and fluffy - about 2 1/2 minutes. Scrape the dough down. Add in eggs, orange and vanilla


extracts. Mix on a low/medium speed until thoroughly combined.


With a tablespoon, scoop out a heaping spoonful of dough (about 3 tablespoons worth or 2 ounces) and place the dough


dollop on parchment paper. The dough will spread, so I suggest only 6 cookies per sheet.


Bake for about 12 minutes. Let cookies cool on a pan before moving them to a plate.


2 cups all-purpose flour


1 teaspoon baking powder


3/4 teaspoon salt


1/2 teaspoon Baking soda


1 1/2 sticks of butter room temperature


1 1/2 cups granulated sugar


3 tablespoons orange zest
