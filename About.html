Why are Aunt Ester's recipes "No; Good!?"


Every time my Aunt Ester would make a dessert, she would say; “It's no good!,


I screwed up the recipe and it tastes wrong.” After that, everyone knew she


had created a savory delicious morsel that would tantalize your taste buds.
