Almond Nougat

Instructions

Ingredients

Line an 8 x 8 inch baking dish with parchment paper.


In a medium size saucepan, combine sugar, cornsyrup, and water. Stir over medium heat until sugar dissolves.


Increase the heat to high, let the mixture boil without stirring until it reaches 290 degrees on the candy thermometer.

br1

While the sugar heats up, beat the egg whites and salt in a large bowl until soft peaks form; you'll know it's ready when


the mixture looks fluffy.


Slowly pour the hot syrup into the egg whites while mixing continuously-take your time here for the best results!


Keep beating until the mixture thickens and turns glossy, and the bowl feels cool to the touch. Add the vanilla extract


during the last stages of mixing.


Transfer the nougat into the lined baking dish, pressing down firmly with a spatula for an even layer.


Once set, remove the nougat from the pan and cut into bite-size pieces using a sharp knife.


2 cups granulated sugar


1 cup light corn syrup


1/4 cup water


2 large egg whites


Pinch of salt


1 teaspoon vanilla extract


1 cup dried cranberries


1 1/2 cups toasted almonds
