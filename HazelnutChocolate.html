Hazelnut Chocolate Crockpot Candy

Instructions

Ingredients

Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.


In a crock pot or slow cooker, add all ingredients except for the salt. Cover and cook on low for an hour, Stirring occasionally


to combine.


Once all the chocolate has melted completely make sure everything is combined.


Using a teaspoon, scoop out the mixture into 1 inch clumps. Sprinkle them with sea salt.


Place in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to harden.


30 ounces dark chocolate chunks


1/2 cup Nutella


2 cups whole roasted hazelnuts (skins removed)


2 cups roasted chopped hazelnuts


Crushed Sea Salt
