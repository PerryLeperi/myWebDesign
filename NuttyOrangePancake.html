Nutty Orange Pancakes

Instructions

Ingredients

In a mixing bowl add all ingredients except the chopped nuts. Mix until combined thoroughly. Fold in chopped nuts.


In a lightly oiled pan, cook like regular pancakes.


Also great in the waffle iron!


1 cup all-purpose flour


1 tablespoon sugar


2 teaspoon baking soda


1/4 teaspoon salt


1/2 teaspoon baking soda


1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon


1 large egg


1 cup orange juice


2 tablespoons cooking oil


1/2 cup finely chopped nuts
