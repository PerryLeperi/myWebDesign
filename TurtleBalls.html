Turtle Balls

Instructions

Ingredients

Mix cream cheese and wafer crumbs.


Shape into 1 inch balls. Freeze for about 30 minutes.


Dip balls in melted chocolate and place on a parchment lined baking sheet.


Drizzle caramel sauce over the top.


Sprinkle with nuts


Refrigerate for about an hour


8 ounces cream cheese, softened


2 1/3 cups Oreo wafer cookies, finely crushed


3 tablespoons caramel ice cream topping


16 ounces semi-sweet baking chocolate, melted


1 cup chopped pecans
